Story

In the far future, noble Houses fight for control of the desert planet Arrakis, home of the spice melange. Paul Atreides must survive insidious politics, giant sandworms, and his own destiny.

World

Arrakis is harsh and unforgiving: endless dunes, powerful storms, and ancient desert tribes. The spice that comes from its sands is the most valuable substance in the universe.

Spice

Melange controls space travel, extends life, and unlocks prescience.

Sandworms

Colossal guardians of the desert, they are both threat and resource for the Fremen.

Fremen

Native fighters adapted to the planet, their survival culture is unmatched.

Characters

Paul Atreides

The heir of House Atreides and the one foretold to bring change to the universe.

Lady Jessica

A Bene Gesserit with loyalty to her son and secret plans of her own.

Baron Harkonnen

Ruthless antagonist determined to crush House Atreides and dominate Arrakis.
